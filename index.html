All About Dogs

These are the dog breed that I love.

Shih Tzu

The Shih Tzu is a toy dog breed originating from Tibet and was bred from the Pekingese and the Lhasa Apso.

Life expectancy:10 to 16 years

Height: 20 to 28 cm (Male, Adult, At the withers), 20 to 28 cm (Female, Adult, At the withers)

Golden Retriever

The Golden Retriever is a Scottish breed of retriever dog of medium size. It is characterised by a gentle and affectionate nature.

Life span:10 to 12 years

Height: Female: 51 to 56 cm, Male: 56 to 61 cm

Colors: Dark Golden, Light Golden, cream, Golden

weight:Female: 25 to 32 kg, Male: 30 to 34 kg

Temperament:Intelligent, Friendly, Reliable, kind, Trustworthy, confident

origin:United Kingdom, Scotland, England

Hypoallergenic:No

Pug

The Pug is a breed of dog originally from China, with physically distinctive features of a wrinkly, short-muzzled face, and curled tail.

origin:China

Colors: Black, Fawn, silver Fawn, Apricot

China Kennel Union: standard
